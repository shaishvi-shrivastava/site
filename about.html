About

I'm a Product Manager who loves creating—both digital products and handmade crafts.

By day, I lead product strategy and ship features that help people. I work cross-functionally with engineering, design, and business teams to build products that solve real problems.

By night, I'm at my craft table—sewing textiles, throwing pottery, knitting blankets, and painting. My work is deeply influenced by my Indian heritage, which brings vibrant colors and intricate patterns into everything I create.

I believe the best products are built with both analytical thinking and creative intuition. Whether I'm conducting user research or designing a pattern for embroidery, I'm driven by the same thing: making something meaningful.
