About

I'm a product manager who loves to solve problems, create meaning, and appreciate beauty.

Currently, I work at Blue River Technology driving global expansion of See & Spray to support farmers, reduce chemical application, and rebuild soils.

In my free time, I love making in all its forms. Whether I'm sewing, knitting, throwing pottery, or painting - I'm inspired by connection, color, and my Indian heritage.

I believe the best products are built with collaboration, creativity, and intention. Whether I'm interviewing customers or mixing paint, I'm driven by these values.
